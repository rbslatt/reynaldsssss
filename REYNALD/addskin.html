Mad Paradox

Add tries to find a way to go back to the past. After much research, he manages to implement a time travel equation to his Dynamos and travels through time. However, he soon discovers that no matter how many times he travels back in time, he can never go back to a reality where he can save his family. This knowledge that he cannot go back to his family eventually drives him insane. Add continues to use the Space-Time Distortion to bring destruction and chaos to his enemies and becomes the demon of paradox!
