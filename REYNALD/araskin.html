Ara

Surya

When Ara borrowed Eun's power, her newfound ability allowed her to see holy beasts. The holy beasts have lost their home and Ara decided that she wanted to help them. In return, the holy beasts lent Ara their powers. Eventually, because she's always trying to help everyone she encounters, the bond between her and the holy beasts begin to weaken. Ara learned that she could not take control of the holy beasts' powers, but instead she should accept that the powers were only lent to her. This understanding led Ara to unlock the spiritual power's true potential. As her journey continues, Ara realized that helping people who suffered from demons is not a deterrent from her goal of chasing Ran. On the contrary, she realized she could help set Ran towards the right path. With the help of the holy beasts, she gains a new understanding of the world. With her full powers unlocked, Ara becomes the all-powerful enlightened guide that lights the path for all creation!
