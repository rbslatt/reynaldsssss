Aisha

Lord Azoth

After feeling great loss due to losing her mana from the ring, Aisha decides that this loss will provide another good opportunity for her to train her magic. She finds out about alchemy in Bethma and jumps at the opportunity to learn about the endless potential alchemy provides as well as its connection to ancient magic. Soon enough she begins to experiment on her body and eventually manages to create a homunculus as well as develop explosive flasks. As she delves more into alchemy, she learns about the Philosopher's Stone and manages to create one after many dangerous experiments. Because of her dedication to her craft, Aisha decides to fuse with the Philosopher's Stone and is reborn as the immortal Lord Azoth! Enemies beware! Her thirst for knowledge cannot be stopped!
