Raven

Revenant

Even after escaping the mind control device and leaving on a journey towards redemption, Raven still feels burning rage within him. In order to completely escape the influence of the mind control device, he decides to destroy the Nasod Arm using Alterasia. The Alterasia successfully destroys his left arm and mutates as the parasitic plant fuses with the Nasod technology. However, the venom from the plant soon begins to spread throughout his body and his vision begins to fade. At the brink of death, Raven is found by the Ponggos and they gift him an armor that slows down the spread of the plant's venom. While the spread did slow down, a large amount of venom collected in his left arm, gaining the ability to instantly turn anything it touched to ashes. With this newfound ability, Raven was reminded of all his precious allies who withered away in vain and makes an oath to stop at nothing to get his revenge!
