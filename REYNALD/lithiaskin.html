Lithia

Achlys

Lithia was gravely mistaken when she thought that everything was going according to plan. It took her friend being hurt for Lithia to realize how dangerous the unknown power beyond the Rift was – she repeated her past mistake. Consumed by regret and loneliness, she tracked down and found the Society to fix her mistakes. Initially feeling insulted when the Society offered information on the Rift in exchange for her cooperation, Lithia relented to the deal for her goals. She will never be able to return to living an ordinary life, so she forges on ahead – even if no answers are to be found. Equipped with the knowledge that there’s no turning back, Lithia has now taken the mantle of Achlys as she embarks on a journey towards the chaotic realm!
